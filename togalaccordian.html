chennai super kings

The Chennai Super Kings (CSK) are a professional cricket team in Chennai, India. They play in the Indian Premier League (IPL) and have won five titles, including 2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, and 2023. The team's home ground is the M.A. Chidambaram Stadium, named after the former BCCI President M.A. Chidambaram
