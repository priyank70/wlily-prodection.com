System Design and Installation Service

Computer consoles, audio equipment and display equipment usually malfunction from time to time. The malfunction can be due to something as simple as a loose connection or burned out fuse or as complicated as a fried circuit board due to a power surge or overloading. Whatever the cause of the malfunction, the technicians at Wiley Production will be happy to troubleshoot your system and identify the underlying issues before they can carry out the necessary repairs. We understand that you have the highest quality equipment, so we take the greatest care while troubleshooting and repairing the system to prevent further damage. We know that there is nothing as bad as hiring an AV repair firm for minor repairs only to end up with a “dead” AV system. That’s why we have hired the most competent technicians to take care of all your AV repair needs.

1. System Design

We understand that every client has different technology needs, so our team usually creates custom solutions to suit the unique needs of each of our clients. Our team normally sits down with the client to come up with technical specifications and system requirements for the new setup. The next step is to come up with a computer aided design using either AutoCAD or any other CAD software out there. This helps to make things clearer for both our client and technical team. We have cloud-based solutions that can help make things easier for your business and boost your performance. Over the years, we have previously installed AV systems in auditoriums, lecture halls, stadiums, homes, apartment buildings, office buildings, offices, conference halls and even homes. All our AV systems are designed to meet international standards and industry best practices, so you can expect to get the best results possible. None of our designs can be considered complete without the client’s input. Our professionals usually make changes and tweaks to address the needs of the client before installation is done.
